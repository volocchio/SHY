Spring Challenge

April 3 – May 2

Our annual Spring Challenge is one of the most anticipated, energetic times at SHY. In celebration of our 10th year, we're changing it up and adding a new optional challenge — the SHY bingo card!

  • Kicks off Friday April 2nd at 11pm
  • Bonus classes & special events added for challengers
  • 4 Saturday workshops to build a solid & safe foundation in all our styles
  • Celebrate your effort at our 10th year social
  • Sign up by March 29th in the studio or online
  • Current packages will be put on hold or extended for the month
  • Not here the whole challenge? No problem — you can have a successful challenge with only 2 or 3 weeks!
