Class Descriptions

All of our classes are open to all levels of ability

Practice with us regularly to decrease stress, heal injuries, increase flexibility, relieve chronic pain, improve strength and stamina, make friends and most importantly… feel amazing, inside and out.

Please alert the instructor of particular concerns and/or conditions you are working with so we can best guide you.

Hot 26

This 26-posture and 2 breathing exercise practice (also known as the "Bikram method") is the original and time-tested therapeutic hot yoga associated with proven health benefits & truly accessible to all ages and bodies.

The repeated, systematic sequence provides a frame of reference to witness the changes within yourself that result from a consistent practice.

Vinyasa

Vinyasa yoga (also known as "flow" yoga) is characterized by sequencing postures together so that you move from one to another, seamlessly, using breath.

Vinyasa is a powerful, energetic class offered in both a warm or hot room. No two classes are ever alike, each session offers a variety of postures building strength and momentum.

Fusion

Fusion yoga classes blend styles and sequences from various yoga styles, most notably the fluid movements in Vinyasa and static postures from the Hot 26 series.

Offered in a warm or hot room, this is a great way to soak up the benefits of both static holds and moving with breath. Suitable for all levels, it will leave you feeling refreshed and restored.

Yin

A soothing, restorative practice composed of long-held postures often using props to relax & rejuvenate. This class is not hot (but heated to a warm temperature) and focuses on a passive approach to target deep connective tissue and joint health.

Yin is an increasingly popular form of yoga to counterbalance the strengthening & muscular work we perform in other styles of yoga & exercise.

Inferno Hot Pilates

A HIIT (high intensity interval training) experience using mat pilates principles, this class strengthens & tones your whole body.

Beginner-friendly with modifications for any body and/or injuries. This is a fun, motivating class with short spurts of high intensity movement followed by rest. We love the way this class complements your yoga practice and illustrates results very quickly!

Yoga Conditioning

A combination of yoga and strength training that uses weights and repetition to build strength and stability. Our class uses familiar yoga poses with added weights and repetition, and may include intervals and Pilates exercises.

We have 2 & 3 lb weights for use, but you are welcome to bring your own. Try it — it's fun!

Ready to Get Started?

All classes are suitable for beginners. Don't know where to start? Just show up!
