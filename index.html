Our Classes

From the classic Hot 26 to flowing Vinyasa and restorative Yin — there's a class for every body.

Hot 26

The original therapeutic hot yoga — 26 postures and 2 breathing exercises in a heated room.

Vinyasa

Powerful, energetic flow yoga linking breath and movement. No two classes are ever alike.

Yin

A soothing, restorative practice of long-held postures to target deep connective tissue.
