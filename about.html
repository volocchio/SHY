About SHY

Keeping It Real

Our Space

It doesn't look like a yoga studio from the outside, but it's what's inside that counts. It was constructed out of love of yoga, community, and creating an unpretentious environment where everyone feels welcome!

We believe in keeping our space and our instruction simple, affordable & available for all.

Studio Info

A few things to know before you visit.

Timeliness

We respect your time & our goal is to start every class on time. Doors open 30 minutes prior to class start. Please arrive at least 5 minutes early. If you sign in and don't show up or cancel within 1 hour of start time, a $10 fee applies.

Parking

We love our neighbors & respect their space. The best place to park is in the middle to far end of the parking lot (closest to the drive-in entrance). Short walk to the front door and leaves more room to get out.

Showers & Changing

We have female and male changing rooms & showers for your convenience and privacy.

Mats & Supplies

We rent mats & towels for $3 each or a mat & towel for $5. We also stock our favorite mats, yoga towels & accessories for purchase at affordable rates!
